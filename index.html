about us

The Sputnik Network is an ecosystem of services that connects Web2 with Web3. Our team create "from-Community-to-Community" tools for quick and easy use of crypto inside messengers and social networks. Users of Twitter and Telegram can receive, send and p2p-exchange crypto in three words or three clicks! For now, Sputnik Network support 16 different coins and tokens from the Cosmos Ecosystem.

use sputnik in twitter

Log In sputnik.exchange with twitter Twitter-account

The bot automatically creates Sputnik-account associated with Twitter-account

Click on deposit and choose one of 16 crypto that you want to use in Twitter

After you top-up balance, send crypto to your friends by adding to your tweet or comment

{@username} {amount}{crypto} #sputnik

Click here for more info!

use sputnik in telegram

start Sputnik Network Bot:https://t.me/SputnikNetworkBot

click on "Deposit" button

click on button of crypto that you want to deposit

you will get address and memo

send crypto of chosen type to this address, and don't forget to input memo!
